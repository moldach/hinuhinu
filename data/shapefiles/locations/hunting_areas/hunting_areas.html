Description:
Abstract:
Public Hunting Areas for the State of Hawaii (Game Birds and Game Mammals) as described in Hawaii Administrative Rules, Title 13, DLNR, Subtitle 5, Forestry and Wildlife, Part 2, Wildlife, Chapters 122 and 123, and as depictd in DOFAW's Hunting Regulation Guides. See <http://dlnr.hawaii.gov/dofaw/files/2013/09/HAR-122-Game-Birds.pdf> and <http://dlnr.hawaii.gov/dofaw/files/2013/09/HAR-123-Game-Mammals.pdf> for legal interpretations.Using the descriptions in the the Hawaii Administrative Rules Title 13, Chapters 122 and 123, the boundaries are based off of State of Hawaii's Tax Map Key (TMK) and Reserves data layer.Sections closed to hunting after the approved 2015 Rules are indicated. New or modified hunting areas after the approved 2015 Rules are not indicated and will be updated on the next Hunting Rules update.
Purpose:
The public hunting area boundaries are a cartographic representation of the Hawaii Administrative Rules Title 13, DLNR, Subtitle 5, Forestry and Wildlife, Part 2, Wildlife, Chapters 122 and 123 Rules Regulating Game Mammal and Game Bird Hunting areas, 2015.
